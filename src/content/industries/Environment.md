As organizations confront the pressing challenges of climate change and environmental issues, effective strategies are essential for sustainable progress. The Climate Change Response Act 2019, commonly known as the Zero Carbon Act establishes a framework for developing and implementing robust climate policies in alignment with New Zealand's commitments under the Paris Agreement.

Our consultancy offers a comprehensive range of services designed to support clients in navigating this complex landscape. Our team of experts excels in:

- **Literature Reviews:** Synthesizing current research to provide a thorough understanding of environmental issues and their implications.
- **Policy Assessments:** Evaluating existing policies to identify strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for enhancement.
- **Decision-Making Under Uncertainty:** Crafting frameworks that enable clients to make informed choices amid ambiguity.
- **Econometrics and Simulation Models:** Utilizing advanced analytical tools to forecast outcomes and inform strategic planning.

We are committed to helping clients prepare for and adapt to the impacts of climate change, contributing to global efforts to limit the increase in average global temperatures to 1.5°C above pre-industrial levels. Our tailored solutions not only assist clients in achieving their environmental targets but also ensure their long-term economic sustainability.
