## Climate Change and the Future of Healthcare

Shifting climate conditions are already transforming healthcare systems. Rising temperatures, extreme weather, and evolving disease patterns are straining infrastructure, workforce capacity, and service delivery. These risks are no longer theoretical, they require strategic, data-driven planning.

We provide analysis on:

- Changing demand from climate-driven health burdens
- System resilience under different climate and policy scenarios
- Infrastructure and workforce adaptation to emerging risks
- Long-term sustainability of healthcare financing and delivery

Our forecasting and scenario modeling pinpoint vulnerabilities and provide the evidence needed to strengthen healthcare resilience.
