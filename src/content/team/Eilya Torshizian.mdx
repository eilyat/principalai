Eilya is the Managing Director of PrincipalAI and the Founding Director of Principal Economics. He leads the development of secure, policy-aware AI systems that combine frontier analytical methods, applied economics, and domain expertise to support better decision-making for governments, infrastructure providers, regulators, and complex organisations.

Eilya brings a rare combination of executive leadership, applied research, economic modelling, and practical AI system development. He has led large multidisciplinary teams across transport, climate, energy, housing, health, regulation, and infrastructure, translating complex evidence into decision-ready advice for Ministers, boards, senior executives, and international organisations. Through PrincipalAI, he is extending this experience into secure AI tools that help organisations synthesise evidence, interrogate data, test scenarios, and produce robust advice.

Before founding Principal Economics and PrincipalAI, Eilya was Principal Economist and Head of the Quantitative Analysis Team at the Institute of Economic Research. Earlier, he worked within a major metropolitan government chief economist function during a period of significant housing and infrastructure pressure, and lectured in economics at the university level. His earlier executive experience includes leading a consumer products manufacturing business and serving as a partner in a diversified holding company active in urban development, construction, information technology, and economic advisory services.

Eilya’s work focuses on using advanced analytical systems to improve the quality, speed, and defensibility of policy and investment decisions. He has led national and international programmes involving emissions budgets, transport appraisal, climate adaptation, infrastructure funding, regulatory pricing, social and economic participation, and decision-making under uncertainty.

At PrincipalAI, Eilya leads the strategic direction of AI products designed for professional advisory environments. His focus is on developing secure, explainable, and evidence-based AI systems that work with organisational knowledge, research libraries, structured data, modelling outputs, and policy documents. These systems are designed to augment expert judgement rather than replace it, helping teams move from large volumes of evidence to clear, auditable, and practical recommendations.

Eilya's interest in intelligent systems predates the recent emergence of large language models. He holds a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology Engineering and has founded and led technology businesses focused on software and algorithm development. Earlier in his career, he established Houshmand Rayan and led the information technology division of Rateb Construction Ltd, overseeing the development of digital products for educational institutions and infrastructure project management. This background provides a strong technical foundation for PrincipalAI's focus on secure, domain-specific AI systems that combine advanced analytics, software engineering, and expert judgement.

Eilya has extensive experience communicating complex technical and economic issues to audiences with different backgrounds. He has led politically sensitive work involving Ministers, government agencies, councils, regulators, monitoring units, and boards, and understands the importance of trust, transparency, privacy, and governance in AI-enabled decision-making.
